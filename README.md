## FAQ

**1) Is this a full VPN or just a proxy?**  
It’s a VPN proxy experience built for Chrome. You connect from the browser and route traffic through encrypted servers.

**2) Does it keep logs of my activity?**  
It follows a **No-Logs policy**. The goal is privacy first, not tracking.

**3) What happens if the connection drops?**  
The **Kill Switch** is designed to stop traffic until the tunnel is back, so you don’t leak requests mid-session.

**4) Will it slow down my browsing?**  
Any VPN adds overhead, but the intent here is **high speed** and a **stable connection**. Choose a nearby server for best results.
